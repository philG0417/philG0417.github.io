Gobble Wobble Vounteer

The Gobble Wobble is a short race for the young who want to be part of the festivities. It involves a quick run around Hillcrest Recreation Center Field. The volunteers help line the route, cheer on the spectators and help keep everyone on track.

Barricade Volunteer

Must be over 18 or with an adult. Requires enthusiastic volunteers to cheer on spectators and keep cars off the route. (Police enforce the main intersections)

Parking Lot Attendent

Enforce the barricades at two of the lots so race participants don't park there.

Post Route Clean up

Drive the route after the race and remove No Parking Signs.
