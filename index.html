About the Turkey Trot

Continue the Tradition

We hope you will continue the tradition and join the fun Thanksgiving morning for the Annual Highland Park Turkey Trot

Come and join friends, family and neighbors in a 5K run around the beautiful Highland Park neighborhood Thanksgiving morning. See the changes taking place at The Highland Bridge. (See route below). Afterwards, stop in at Tiffany's Sports Lounge for a FREE breakfast for all participants and a free Bloody Mary for all participants twenty one years and older!
