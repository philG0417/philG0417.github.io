About the Turkey Trot

Join in the fun!

Plan to join friends, family and neighbors in a 5K run around the beautiful Highland Park neighborhood Thanksgiving morning. (See route below)

Not a runner? Plan to bring the kids to the Gobble Wobble- a short run around Hillcrest Park.

Afterwards, stop in at Tiffany's Sports Lounge for a FREE breakfast for all participants and a free Bloody Mary for all participants twenty one years and older.

FAQ

Where and When is Race Packet Pickup?

Tuesday, November 21st: Highland Catholic (Main Entrance on Bohland Street) 2:00-5:30 p.m. .....

Is There Same Day Registration?

Yes! Race day registration will be open at 7:30 at the race start. Fee on race day for the 5K is $35 for adults, $25 for 14 and under. Gobble Wobble is $10, additional siblings $5

Is Parking Available?

Parking is limited. Please plan to walk or carpool and obey all no parking signs. Be advised there is no parking in the church or school lot or the lot on the south side of Ford Parkway.

Are Shirts Guaranteed?

Shirts are not guaranteed. In order to receive a shirt, you must register by November 10th. If shirts are not picked up by the start of the race, any extras will be distributed on a first come first serve basis after the race begins.

How Does The Free Breakfast Work?

Tiffany's offers a complementary breakfast for all race participants (and Bloody Mary for all over 21) starting at 8:00 am. Your bib is your ticket to the breakfast.

What if I don't run but want to join others for breakfast?

All runners receive the free breakfast with their bib, if you do not run, you can pay $10 at the door of Tiffany's and get your breakfast.
